Distinguished by slender grains that elongate up to twice their original size when cooked. Distinct delicate, nutty and slightly floral aroma — "Basmati" means "fragrant" in Hindi. Aged 1–2 years to reduce moisture, intensifying aroma and improving elongation. Fluffy, light, and non-sticky — each grain remains separate. Available in white, parboiled, and brown variants.

Our Fertilizer Range

Three categories of premium organic soil enhancers

Vermicompost
Nutrient-rich organic fertilizer produced by earthworms (red wiggler worms) breaking down organic waste in 75–90 days. Superior, nutrient-dense castings that boost plant growth, improve soil structure, aeration, and water retention. Rich in enzymes, microbes, and essential nutrients (N, P, K, Ca, Mg). Reduces risk of pests, diseases, and parasitic nematodes. Eco-friendly, odorless, chemical-free.
Pest RepellentSoil AerationChemical-Free75–90 Day Process
Cow Dung Manure
100% organic, sun-dried, and pulverized fertilizer called "Black Gold" — rich in nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. Fully aged and decomposed, making it odorless and safe for immediate use without burning plant roots. Improves soil porosity, moisture retention, and acts as a natural pesticide. Sun-dried and often sourced from Desi cows. Ideal for potting mixes to improve nutrient content and moisture retention.
Sun-DriedDesi Cow SourceFine PowderNo Root Burn
Bio-Fertilizers
Eco-friendly products containing living microorganisms — bacteria, fungi, or algae — that improve soil fertility and promote plant growth by increasing nutrient availability. Key types: nitrogen-fixing (Rhizobium, Azotobacter), phosphorus-solubizing, and VAM fungi. Enhance yield by 10–20%, reduce reliance on chemical fertilizers, and improve soil health sustainably. Non-polluting and help restore natural soil ecosystems.
