Who We Are

Chennai's Premier
Agri Export Partner

"We don't just ship spices — we carry the flavour of India to the world."

Founded in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Foreign Bridge Export & Import Corp specialises in exporting India's finest spices, agricultural commodities, and organic products — premium red & green chillies in 14+ varieties, robusta bananas, turmeric powder, cashew nuts, cardamom, basmati rice, pepper, and organic fertilizers — to discerning buyers across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas.

From farm sourcing and ASTA colour grading to APEDA documentation and international freight, our end-to-end approach ensures every shipment meets your exacting quality standards.

Our Journey

From Chennai to Global Markets

Foreign Bridge began as a small trading firm with a single mission: to bring the authentic taste of Indian spices to the world. Over 15 years, we have grown into a full-service export corporation handling over 8 product categories, serving buyers across 30+ countries.

Our Chennai roots give us unparalleled access to South India's finest produce — from the fiery chillies of Guntur to the fragrant cardamom of the Western Ghats. Every batch we export carries decades of agricultural tradition and the commitment of the farmers who grow it.
